Privacy Policy — Estimation

Last Updated: December 16, 2025

1) Who We Are

Estimation ("The App") is a card game application developed by TGR.

Contact: estimation.kings.tgr@gmail.com

2) Overview

This policy explains what information we collect when you use the app, how we use it, how we share it, and your rights regarding your data.

3) Information We Collect

A) Account & Auth Data

  • When signing in (via Email, Google, or Guest), we create a user record in our database.
  • We may store basic information like your User ID, Email (if provided), and Username.

B) Gameplay & Stats

  • We collect game results, win/loss ratios, XP/RP levels, and in-game currency balances to maintain your profile and leaderboard status.

C) Device & Diagnostics

  • We may collect non-personal technical data such as device type, OS version, and crash logs to improve app stability.

D) Ads Data

  • We use Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect data such as Advertising IDs to provide personalized or non-personalized ads based on your settings.

4) How We Use Information

  • To operate the game (authentication, saving progress, leaderboards).
  • To improve user experience and fix bugs.
  • To show advertisements via Google AdMob.
  • To manage in-app rewards (e.g., gems for watching ads).

5) Third Party Services

We use the following third-party services which may process your data according to their own privacy policies:

  • Supabase: For database hosting and authentication. Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob: For advertising. Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Services: For app distribution and payments.

6) Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your data as long as your account is active. You have the right to delete your account and all associated data at any time.

How to delete your data:

  • In-App: Go to Settings → Delete Account.
  • Web Request: Email us at estimation.kings.tgr@gmail.com.

7) Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
